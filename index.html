about

Hello! My name is John and I'm a software engineer in Brooklyn, NY. I currently work remotely for an Atlanta-based startup called Insiten where my kick-ass team and I build software for businesses.

I love the web in its entirety, but I am especially passionate about the front-end side of development. My favorite aspect of the field is the intersection of design and technology. I am constantly learning, building, and participating in the dev community both online and in person.

Besides web development, I also enjoy Spanish, travel, eating, and salsa dancing.
